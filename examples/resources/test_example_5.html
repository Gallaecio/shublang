Biography

Shani Greene-Dowdell is a wife, mother of three, grandmother to two awesome tots, and has worked 18 years as a medical transcriptionist. Shani enjoys weaving words of romantic fiction and suspense and has written her way into a new reality as a full-time writer. Shani has 20 books to her credit in Interracial Romance, Erotica, and Urban genres.

You can find Shani Greene-Dowdell online @ www.shanigreenedowdell.com or email her anytime to chat books at shanibooks@gmail.com.
